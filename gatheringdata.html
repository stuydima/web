I always start off by gathering data from a request made by someone who requires a graphic for whatever purpose. They provide me with an idea and (optionally) instructions for what I should do. Usually I'm just given a prompt and I have to determine what to do with it myself. What helps me is looking at my past creations in Photoshop and getting inspiration from there. It helps me preserve a consistent graphical style.


If I am simply creating something for my own pleasure during leisure time, I always use references for whatever I'm trying to make. Not only do references help me make more proportionally accurate results, they also help me make more visually appealing designs.
