Ever wonder what it's like to delve into the field of creating digital art for others to use and view? Look no further, for I'm here to tell you everything about graphic design! In this article, we'll delve into my personal process behind the creation of graphics for an organization and during my leisure time.

I create graphics in the pixel art style. Pixel art originated from primitive display technology and became recognized from classic arcade games. To this day, games are created in this style to embrace the aesthetic of a limited resolution while still creating recognizable images. More recently, digital art began to be made in pixel art as well due to its simplicity and easy-to-learn techniques (but hard to master).
