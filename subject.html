The Process and Journey of Making Graphics

I have been making graphics for a certain company for almost a year now. The process itself is rather painstaking and time-consuming, but it in itself is quite entertaining and occupies me with something to do. I use Adobe Photoshop to make the graphics.

  • Gathering Data
  • Formulating Ideas
  • Bringing to Fruition

Gathering Data

I always start off by gathering data from a request made by someone who requires a graphic for whatever purpose. They provide me with an idea and (optionally) instructions for what I should do. Usually I'm just given a prompt and I have to determine what to do with it myself. What helps me is looking at my past creations in Photoshop and getting inspiration from there. It helps me preserve a consistent graphical style.

Formulating Ideas

This is where the creativity part comes in: I have to determine what to do with the prompt I'm given. The only limit is your imagination, so usually I make a few concepts up in my head and then work from there in Photoshop .

Bringing to Fruition

This is often the trickiest part; it takes lots of patience which I lack. It's also where skill is most utilized so it often takes me several tries for what I'm making to resemble what I'm seeing in my head as an image. Photoshop's interface was not very tricky to figure out, so it mostly comes down to what I can do with the Pencil Tool
